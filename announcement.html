About the PyTorch Foundation

The PyTorch Foundation is a neutral home for the AI/ML and deep learning community to collaborate on the open source PyTorch framework and ecosystem. The PyTorch Foundation is supported by leading contributors to the PyTorch open source project and leverages resources provided by members and contributors to enable community discussions and collaboration.
